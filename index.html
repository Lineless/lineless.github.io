Lineless


Welcome to Lineless, a web app designed to remove all waiting in restaurants and delicatessens.

How does it work?

  1. Find a restaurant that is a partner of Lineless
  2. Buy the food you want and pay for it
  3. When the order is almost done, you will receive a text message to notify you

Why use it?

Save money

Know how much you will spend ahead of time.

Spend money easily

Using Lineless is much more streamlined and simpler than other ways to pay.

Secure spending

I can totally guarantee that this is super duper secure.

Save time

Lineless was designed to help you spend more time on the things that matter most.
